About

At GreenFinder we are passionate about spreading the game we love, golf. Our key focus is to make the game of golf much more accessible to spread the joy of golf to a much wider audience. We accomplish this goal by helping raise awareness of golf courses that are available to the general public. We also depend on our community to help review courses to let golfers make infomed decisions on new courses to play.
